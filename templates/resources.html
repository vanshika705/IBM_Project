Publications

World Health Statistics 2024

This annual report by the World Health Organization (WHO) compiles health-related indicators from the SDGs and discusses global health trends, issues, and statistics. It provides insights into key health metrics and tracks progress towards health-related SDGs.

The Sustainable Development Goals Report 2024

Published by the United Nations Statistics Division, this report offers a comprehensive overview of global progress towards the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development. It includes data and analyses from over 50 international agencies and more than 200 countries, covering health and education indicators among others.

2024 Progress Report on the Global Action Plan for Healthy Lives and Well-being for All (SDG3 GAP)

This WHO report focuses on alignment and collaborative efforts to achieve health-related SDGs, emphasizing country-level impacts and strategies for enhanced implementation.

Research on Health Policy and Systems (HPSR) Priorities

A detailed research paper that explores the implications of SDGs for health policy and systems research, highlighting themes such as social protection, multisectoral collaboration, and participatory health institutions. It includes findings from a global priority-setting exercise for health policy and systems research.

Toolkits

WHO Toolkit on Climate Change and Health

The World Health Organization (WHO) has launched a new toolkit aimed at empowering health professionals to address the health impacts of climate change. This toolkit provides comprehensive resources to help health workers understand the health impacts of climate change and communicate effectively about the co-benefits of climate action.

SDG3 Global Action Plan

The 2024 progress report on the Global Action Plan for Healthy Lives and Well-being for All (SDG3 GAP) focuses on enhancing alignment among agencies and countries to strengthen health systems. It includes country case studies that showcase collaboration efforts to achieve health-related SDG targets.

Global Health Observatory (GHO)

WHO's GHO provides extensive data and resources related to the SDGs, including indicators for monitoring health outcomes. This platform can be used to access data, publications, and tools relevant to SDG3 and other health-related goals.
