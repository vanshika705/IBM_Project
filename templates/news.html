nited Nations SDG Report 2024

UN Secretary-General António Guterres highlighted significant challenges in meeting SDG targets, emphasizing that only 17% of the goals are on track. However, he noted progress in areas such as gender parity in education and the reduction of HIV infections. He called for accelerated actions in peacebuilding, green transitions, and financial support to enhance SDG achievements.

WHO Global Report on Menstrual Health and Hygiene

Released on Menstrual Hygiene Day, this report by WHO and UNICEF underscores the gaps in menstrual health education and facilities in schools globally. It reveals that only 39% of schools provide menstrual health education and stresses the need for better access to menstrual products and adequate facilities for girls, particularly in low-income regions.
